1.PLANNING INFORMATION
A.Effectivity
* * * CF34-10A
This Service Bulletin is applicable to these CF34-10A engines:
•CF34-10A engines, serial numbers 623109 through 623116.

This Service Bulletin has been introduced in production to these CF34-10A engines:
•CF34-10A engines, serial numbers 623117 and up.

These serial numbers are the best available data.
The shoulder bolt P/N 2133M17P01 is affected by this Service Bulletin.
B.Description
This Service Bulletin replaces the current aft mount failsafe shoulder bolt with an improved shoulder bolt P/N 2431M79P01.
C.Compliance
Category 2
GE recommends that you do this Service Bulletin prior to engine revenue service.
NOTE: This Service Bulletin can be accomplished on wing or in shop.
This Service Bulletin is offered to improve the reliability or performance of your GE product, or to help prevent the occurrence of the event or condition described in this Service Bulletin. If the operator elects not to participate in the bulletin, that decision will be taken into consideration by GE in evaluating future product performance issues that may arise in the operator's fleet.
D.Concurrent Requirements
None.
E.Reason
(1)Objective:
To replace the current aft mount failsafe shoulder bolt with the improved shoulder bolt. The redesigned shoulder bolt meets the inspection interval design goal for the mount system.
(2)Condition:
The old shoulder bolt does not meet inspection interval design requirements.
(3)Cause:
The geometry and size of the bolt shank fillet head results in lower than required low cycle fatigue (LCF) capability.
(4)Improvement:
The new bolt has a larger diameter wrenching feature, a larger radius, and a taller bolt head, resulting in improved LCF capability. The new bolt meets the inspection interval design goal.
(5)Substantiation:
Substantiation is by analysis.
F.Approval
This Service Bulletin has been reviewed by the FAA and the repair(s) and modification(s) herein comply with the applicable Federal Aviation Regulations and are FAA APPROVED for installation in the model(s) listed in this Service Bulletin.
G.Manpower
After you get access to the shoulder bolt, you will need approximately 2 man-hours to do the replacement portion of this Service Bulletin.
H.Weight and Balance
The complete compliance with this Service Bulletin increases weight by 0.23 lb (0.10 kg).
